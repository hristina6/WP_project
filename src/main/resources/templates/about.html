About Us

We are a group of students passionate about making survey creation and management easy and accessible. Our mini survey app is designed to help you create surveys, collect responses, and analyze results effortlessly.

"The goal is to turn data into information, and information into insight." - Carly Fiorina

Did you know? The first known survey was conducted in 1086, known as the Domesday Book. Today, surveys are a powerful tool for gathering insights from diverse audiences.
